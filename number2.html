Top 8 mango recipes

mango cheesecake

Mango has a unique ability to instantly lighten a cheesecake and balance it out. This no-bake recipe is easy to make, has a simple biscuit base and requires no fancy ingredients. Try adding a drizzle of passionfruit pulp for the ultimate tropical dessert.

mango salad

Mangoes instantly add a touch of fanciness to any salad, and this recipe is a perfect example of how you can use them to jazz up a simple recipe. Paired up with baby spinach, avocado and bacon, this is quick to make and ideal to take along to a barbecue.

Merry Mango Trifle

One thing's for sure - mango and trifle are a match made in food heaven! Made with mango, fruit cake and cream, it is incredibly simple to put together and can quickly whipped up when unexpected guests call in.

Coconut mango cake

Mangoes are ideal in baked goods, and this cake is the perfect example of where they shine. Light and fluffy, it can be made with either fresh or tinned fruit and is ideal served with a pot of tea.

Mango sauce

The sweetness from the mango paired with the savouriness of the crumbed chicken is an unexpected combination that packs a real punch of flavour.

mango salsa

If you're after a refreshing side to serve alongside grilled meat or fish, you can't beat this mango salsa. Ideal served with prawns, chicken or anywhere you'd like a little bit of zing and a big tropical flavour.

mango popsicles

This recipe is the ultimate way to eat mangoes when it's too hot for dessert. These frozen popsicles can be made up well ahead of time and pulled out of the freezer on a hot day when you don't feel like cooking but want something sweet and refreshing to indulge in.

mango termisu

This super simple dessert is only made better with the addition of fresh mango chunks.
